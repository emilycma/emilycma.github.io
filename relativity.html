Stage Separation Bracket //

stage 2 vehicle structures, 2024

Terran R is a multistage rocket. Stage separation occurs once the vehicle reaches initial parking orbit. I was responsible for the initial design scoping of the stage 2 separation bracket, intended to fly with Terran R in 2026. I collected requirements from relevant systems and presented to stakeholders. These requirements informed my intial design and sizing. Further sizing was completed with finite element software. This culminated in a final review of my analysis. The results presented will be recapitulated in the critical design review for this component. I defended my design decisions with FEM stress plots, bolt bush forces, lug and bolted joint margin calculations, I was the lead of these design meetings, and I was the responsible engineer for this component.

I am thankful to my mentor for his guidance, knowledge, and support while giving me freedom to own my design and analysis decisions.
