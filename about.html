My name is Emily, and I am a mechanical engineer with interests in structures and materials design and analysis. I have four years of experience on a Formula SAE team, holding leadership positions as chassis lead and on the executive team. I have gained skills in full-cycle design, build, and validation of structural components of our formula-style racecar. I continued structural design work at Relativity Space, where I brought a critical function stage separation component from initial design criteria scoping through final analysis review.

I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering from Washington University in St. Louis in May 2023. I am pursuing my Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University with an anticipated graduation in September 2025. My research as part of the Gu Research Group involves design and analysis of topology-optimized soft magnets using laser powder bed fusion.

I am looking for internships Spring 2025 and full time opportunities starting Fall 2025.
